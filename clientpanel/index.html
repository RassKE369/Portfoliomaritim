About Me

My tech journey began in a small local cyber café, where I learned the basics of technology through real, hands-on work. From helping clients with eCitizen and KRA services to designing posters and troubleshooting computers, I discovered how much I enjoyed solving problems and making tech work for people.

After my time at Cyber Staff, I joined Kirinyaga University to pursue Software Engineering, driven purely by passion and curiosity. I wanted to understand how the tools I’d been using were built — and how I could create my own. That decision completely changed how I see technology and the impact it can have.

Today, I’m focused on growing as a software developer, exploring web development, automation, and creative tech projects. I love blending design and code to build practical, user-friendly solutions — and I’m always excited to learn, collaborate, and create something new!
